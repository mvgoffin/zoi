Frequently asked questions

Zoi partners with environmentally conscious companies that offer electronics and office furniture that are in good condition, but redundant. We offer a convenient and easy way to get access to these devices and furniture for your social programmes.

To get started, you need to register your beneficiary account, then select the product and quantity you'd like to receive. Finally, you need to complete the Request for Donation Form at checkout. Once reviewed and accepted by the Zoi team, we'll let you know, so you're ready to receive the donation.

Reusing is better than recycling because it saves the energy that comes with having to dismantle and re-manufacture products. It also significantly reduces waste and pollution because it reduces the need for raw materials, saving both forests and water supplies.

In the UK, the market benefits from reuse thanks to an influx of quality products at reduced prices. After the Greater London Authority introduced reusing in their sustainability plan a few years ago, 12,000 tonnes of goods were reused in 2013.

Each product goes through a detailed quality check and multi-stage processing, so that each device offered from Zoi arrives in good condition to reuse. If the performance isn’t as expected, our customer service is happy to help.

All the products listed are donations. You'll only need to pay for the shipping costs and a commission of 2% of total transaction. This fee helps operate the Zoi platform, including 24/7 customer service and credit card processing.

At checkout, we will ask you to complete our Request for Donation Form that includes 2 main questions; why do you need the donation and what is your e-waste strategy. This ensures that everyone has visibility of the impact we're making.
