Gubel is an ethical consumer brand founded by Marco Valle, a self‚Äîtrained chocolatier from Belgium that thought about blending his passion for chocolate and the calming benefits he has experienced from green tea.

Since late 2018, he spent a year developing a heritage-inspired recipe of dark chocolate hazelnut truffles that contain a velvety ganache infused with L-theanine, an amino acid found in green tea, with the mission to help people curb stress and feel more balanced in an indulgent and healthy way.
